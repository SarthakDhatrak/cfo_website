About Sanjeev

Who I Am

I am Sanjeev Singh, a B.Tech CSE professional specializing in Cybersecurity & Blockchain, and the Co-Founder & Chief Financial Officer (CFO) at Spotwon Group, a diversified technology and consumer enterprise building multiple business verticals across India.

With a strong foundation in cyber defence, ethical security practices, blockchain application architecture, and secure digital ecosystems, I am focused on contributing to India’s technological and cybersecurity advancement. As CFO of Spotwon Group, I oversee financial strategy, capital allocation, operational planning, and strategic growth initiatives, ensuring scalable expansion, financial stability, and long-term value creation across our business portfolio.

I am committed to leadership excellence, disciplined execution, and data-driven decision-making, and I believe in empowering innovation and building sustainable organizations that strengthen India’s digital future.
